What is#WePlant?

#WePlant is a non-profit campaign where we believe in the restoration of healthy earth, by planting trees. But firstly, what is Restoration?

What is Restoration?

Restoration is the action of returning something to a former owner, place, or condition. For example, a rusted iron nail can be restored into a clean, shiny iron object that can be used for decorative purposes.

Goal 1: Plant 1,000,000 trees by the end of 2023

We want to plant 1,000,000 trees within the next 14-15 months.

Goal 2: Reduce the effects of Climate Change

The more trees we plant the more we reduce OUR Carbon Footprint.

Goal 3: Over 100,000 supporters to our cause

We want over 100,000 to share their contributions and follow our cause.
