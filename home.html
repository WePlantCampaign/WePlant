Restore Oxygen with #WePlant

WELCOME TO THE FAQ SECTION!

#WePlant is a non-profit campaign where we believe in the restoration of healthy earth, by planting trees. In the next section the answer to your questions about us will soon be answered.

Who is #WePlant made for?

Anybody can contribute to our cause. We care about you and the environment.

What is the #WePlant Campaign made up of?

Whether you chose to plant a tree or a forest, your contribution matters. With out your contributions, our cause will have no meaning.

Why should I plant a tree?

The global warming crisis has resulted in a higher concentration of Carbon Dioxide in the atmosphere. Through reforestation, we can increase the concentration of oxygen in the environment. Did you know ONE tree can provide a day's supply of oxygen for up to four people...

Can planting trees solve climate change?

NO, not alone. Reforestation may solve small problemns but the main impacts of climate change may stay for the next millenium. Maybe the 50th Generation of you may be free from climate change, but only if YOU PLANT TREES.

Is there any catch to joining #WePlant?

#WePlant is a non-profit campaign. There is no catch joining, and it's completely free.

How to be part of #WePlant?

Get a tree seed, find a open soil area and plant your seed there. Provide it with water. In 10 years you would have a tree that would have provided oxygen to more than 5 million people. That's about the population of Singapore.
